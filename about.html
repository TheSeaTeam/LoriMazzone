About Lori Mazzone


Hi, I'm Lori! Thank you for visiting my page! I was born in Texas, but have lived for many years in beautiful Florida, and I have found my true passion in Real Estate. I am a full-time, trained certified professional Realtor® with Optima Real Estate, licensed since 2014. I specialize in New Construction, and Custom-built Luxury Homes in Winter Park, College Park and Orlando.

As a multimillion-dollar producer, I will provide you with expert skills in marketing, presentations and negotiations. I have a powerful personality and my goal is to build strong relationships with the people that I meet, and make those connections last a lifetime. I pledge a strict Code of Ethics, honesty, and a wonderful attitude. I have knowledge of the current market trends and I get a strong sense of fulfillment by offering my expertise to others.

My husband and I enjoy the quiet company of our beautiful dogs and horses. I enjoy horseback riding, golfing, reading, going to the beach, and swimming with our dogs. If you would like to sell your home, buy a new home, or build a custom home, please contact me directly at 407-301-2474, or email me at lorimazzonesells@gmail.com. Let’s have some fun!!
