About Lori Mazzone


I was born in Texas, and I have found my true passion in Real Estate. I am a full time, trained certified professional Realtor® with Optima Real Estate, licensed since 2014. I specialize in New Construction, and Custom-built Luxury Homes in Winter Park, College Park and Orlando. As a multimillion-dollar producer, I will provide you with expert skills in marketing, presentations and negotiations. I have a powerful personality and my goal is to build strong relationships with the people that I meet, and make those connections last a lifetime. I pledge a strict Code of Ethics, am honest and I have a great attitude. I have knowledge of the current market trends and I get a great sense of fulfillment by offering my expertise to others. My husband and I enjoy the quiet company of our beautiful dogs and horses. I enjoy horseback riding, golfing, reading, going to the beach, and swimming with our dogs.
